Dominican Republic

The Dominican Republic boasts over 800 miles of coastline, featuring world-renowned beaches with fine white sand, turquoise waters, and swaying coconut palms. Popular spots include the bustling resorts of Bávaro Beach and Punta Cana, the dramatic, secluded shores of Samaná, and the wind-swept northern coast ideal for watersports. With numerous Blue Flag certifications, these beaches offer a mix of high-energy water activities, tranquil relaxation, and stunning natural beauty.
